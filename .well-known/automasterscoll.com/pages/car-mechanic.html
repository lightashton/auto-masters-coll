Customer Satisfaction is Our Top Priority

Get in touch! We can fix your automobile at your convenience.

At Yaffo Auto Service, keeping our customers safe while they are driving on the busy roadways is important to us. When an automobile owner looks for a car mechanic around the Alsip area, they want to find someone that they can not only trust to do repairs the right way on the first try but one that charges fair rates and treats them with the dignity and respect they deserve. When you make the informed decision to partner with our team, you get all of this and more. We only hire mechanics who are not only certified to work on your automobile but ones whose core values and personality fit the culture that we have developed over the years to treat our customers like they’re a member of our own family. Whether you are getting a wheel alignment or another service done on your car, we are here for you.

Our garage only installs parts on your vehicle that meet or exceed the original equipment manufacturer (OEM) standards. This helps ensure your vehicle’s needs (and yours) are met. We always work to ensure that every repair you trust us with makes your automobile as safe and reliable as it was when you drove it for the first time. During our 15 years of helping our customers with their car repair needs, Yaffo Auto Service has built a big reputation of trust with our customers. When you entrust your vehicle with our team, you will enjoy high-quality service from friendly mechanics who aren’t trying to rip you off. No one likes being upsold on services or repairs that their auto simply doesn’t need, and you will never encounter that attitude here. Our available services include tune-ups, tire rotation and balancing, oil changes, brake services, 30 / 60 / 90 K services, and more. Drop by the garage or give us a call with any questions that you have for our team, and we look forward to taking care of your vehicle today.
