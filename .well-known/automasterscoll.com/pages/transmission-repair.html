Transmission Repair

Customer Satisfaction is Our Top Priority

Get in touch! We can fix your automobile at your convenience.

If you drive a vehicle that is more than a few years old, you will probably have to seek out a reputable shop to help you with a transmission repair at some point. This holds if you live around the Alsip, Orland Park, Oak Lawn, Chicago Ridge, and Palos Heights areas of metro-Chicago where we see more than our fair share of stop and go traffic. Once you think you might have an issue with the transmission, you should give the team a call at Yaffo Auto Service. Throughout the more than 15 years of experience that we have in the industry, we have found that car owners who take early action on these types of issues are the ones who avoid a more costly transmission rebuild project later. No matter what, when you bring your vehicle into our garage to have our expert technicians diagnose the exact transmission issues that you are having with your auto, you can be comfortable in the knowledge that we will never steer you wrong when it comes to only fixing what has to be fixed on your auto to get it back up and running.

We take great pride in our customer service, and this has been a focus of ours since we first opened our doors. That is why we always have an ASE certified technician working on your vehicle so that the job gets done right on the first try without fail. If you have had your transmission fail on you to the point that you can’t drive it into our garage safely, just call our towing service. If it is after hours, they will drop your vehicle off at the shop, and we’ll take a look at everything for you on the next day that we are open. Our goal is to get your auto back up and running as soon as possible. If we see that there are different service options for you to consider after the technician gives the auto a thorough diagnostic check, we will review this with you before we move forward with repairs. Yaffo Auto Service believes in 100% transparency in every task and thing that we do. If you aren’t quite sure what needs to be done to fix your car or truck that is OK too. Either call or stop by, and we will review your options with you.
