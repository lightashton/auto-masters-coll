Engine Rebuilding

Customer Satisfaction is Our Top Priority

Get in touch! We can fix your automobile at your convenience.

There are a variety of indicators that may show your automobile needs an engine rebuilding service. These range from simply hearing loud noises coming from under the vehicle’s hood when running the engine to the automobile using oil too quickly. If the vehicle is leaking oil, this could cause significant damage to the engine. This problem can be caused by worn valve guides, stuck oil rings, and other issues within the system. If you are looking for a reputable auto mechanic to help you with these and other engine repair needs, then you should make it a point to reach out to the team at Yaffo Auto Service today.

Our company has more than 15 years of experience, and we serve customer’s automobiles from the Alsip, Orland Park, Oak Lawn, Chicago Ridge, and Palos Heights areas. After we run a full diagnostic on your car’s engine, we will take a methodical approach to the rebuild process and only replace the parts that no longer meet the manufacturer’s specifications for your vehicle. When we start replacing components, if we discover further problems with the engine head off like excessively worn cylinders with loose pistons, then we will lift the entire engine block out. This is so our certified technician can reach the cylinders to bore and hone them fully so they can be fitted for a new piston. We’ll also replace other required components like those required for timing, the main and rod bearings, freeze plugs, and anything else requiring replacement. Once the job is complete, the rebuilt engine should last for the life of your car as long as you maintain good maintenance habits for the vehicle. If you do not, then it will just be a matter of time before the issues arise again.

Our technicians will explain everything to you, and we will be happy to help take care of your recurring maintenance needs as well. If you have any questions, please reach out and give us a call today. Getting an engine rebuilt is a big process, but you have found the right team to take care of you. We look forward to seeing you come by the garage.
