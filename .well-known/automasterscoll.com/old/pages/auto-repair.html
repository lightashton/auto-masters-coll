Auto Repair

Yaffo Auto Service has been delivering high-quality auto repair service to the residents of Alsip, Illinois and the surrounding cities for many years now. Our garage is a full-service shop, and we have more than 15 years of experience helping our customers with minor and major repairs on all makes and models of both foreign and domestic automobiles. Every technician that works for our team is ASE certified, and we use the latest in automotive technology to accurately identify problems with our customer’s vehicles and deliver the highest quality repairs for the best possible price.

Our ASE certified technicians will never let you down, and we will always give you our recommendations on what the next periodic maintenance task for your auto that you should be tracking is as well as the different repair plans available to you for getting corrective maintenance complete and your auto back on the road again for you.

We always act with integrity and honesty, and we work with you to get the work done in your car as quickly as possible so that you can get back on the road. If your vehicle needs a body shop, we do that too. There is nothing that our team can’t handle for you. We understand what a major investment a vehicle is for most people, and that is why we treat your car like we were working on our own. We offer complimentary quotes for customers who have their car’s serviced with our garage, and we accept all major credit cards, cash, and checks for payment.

We founded Yaffo Auto Service on the basic idea that all customers deserve respect, and you will see that from our team every day. This small touch has resulted in our loyal family of local customers, and we look forward to helping you as well. Our garage offers the full range of car repair services to include brake service, oil changes, transmission rebuilds, engine repair, and more. We will always give you options when there are ones available for fixing your vehicle, and our technicians will never pressure you into choosing unnecessary or extra services for your automobile. If you have any questions regarding repairs needed for your car, truck, van, or SUV, please drop by the garage or give us a call. We look forward to hearing from you.
