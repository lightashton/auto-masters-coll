Engine Repair

It happens to all of us at some point in owning an automobile. The check engine light will come on, and this feeling of angst will start to come on. Should you wait until you start hearing noises to bring your automobile into a mechanic? Or should you go ahead and do so now before the potential issue with your engine gets worse? Here at Yaffo Auto Service, we recommend that you give us a call to 1 – Get the reason for the check engine light, and 2 – Run a complete engine performance check if required to determine if you need to pursue an engine repair. Our highly skilled and certified technicians will make sure that we drill down into everything our diagnostic check reveals about your automobile, and then we’ll review your options with you. Many times a check engine light will simply reveal an issue with one of the many sensors in today’s automobiles like the O2 sensor or something else which are easy fixes for our technicians to handle for you. If our diagnostic check indicates that additional work is required to repair the engine, we’ll give you a thorough run-down. Our goal is to avoid the more costly engine replacement on your vehicle if possible, and we will talk you through our options. Some of the signs to look for if you suspect your auto needs an engine repair are: starting troubles, shaking or strange noises from beneath the hood of the car while the engine is running, gas mileage is getting worse and worse, idling and stalling, and more. A key to minimizing the overall impact on both your pocketbook and availability of your vehicle is to give us a call as soon as you start to notice problems with the vehicle. The sooner we can get to diagnosing the issue and fixing it, the sooner that you are back up and running with a fully functional automobile.
