About Us

From what started as a small family business, Auto Master’s collision has grown into one of the biggest auto body repair shops in Las Vegas, providing auto body repair, collision repair, car paint repair and custom auto paint services. For over 2 decades we have been the lead provider of high-quality automobile body repairs on a nationwide scale, with repeatable results and industry-leading customer satisfaction scores. On qualifying repairs, we provide a nationwide warranty as well as a service guarantee.

At the core of our business is a strong desire to satisfy our customers. To this end, we have tailored our services to always cater for our customers' needs. With affordable auto body repair packages and an option to opt in for a mobile auto body repair where our collision repair experts come to meet you at any location of your choice to get your car fixed, you can trust that your car would be fixed with your convenience in mind.Our auto body shop in Las Vegas is fitted with the latest technology in auto body and collision repair. The goal is to get your car looking as good as new even after a crash. We apply modern techniques and have only expert hands on the job.

Our team of technicians are ICar certified and have over 3 decades of experience between them. Our expert technicians are also familiar with a wide range of car brands and models. We don’t stop at just collision repair, we also offer car paint repair and full on auto painting services. We set ourselves apart by providing a hassle-free 'one-stop' automotive repair service that is focused on delivering all automotive body repair services in Las Vegas at non-negotiable value.

Our Mission

To provide customers in Las Vegas with top rated, high-quality, and convenient one-stop auto repair services at affordable prices in a well-equipped, modernized auto body shop, complemented by mobile auto body repair services that provide customers the option of location based services for a select range of car body repairs and maintenance.

Our Focus

To remain a top rated service provider for auto body and collision body repairs in Las Vegas and its surrounding areas.
