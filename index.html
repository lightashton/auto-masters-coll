Preserving Your Safety

Your windshield does more than just shield you from the elements; it plays a vital role in keeping you safe on the road. In the event of an accident, it provides structural support to prevent the roof from caving in, and it aids in the correct deployment of airbags. A properly installed windshield ensures that you and your passengers are safe in the event of a collision.

Maintaining Visibility

Clear visibility on the road is paramount for safe driving. A windshield that's incorrectly installed may lead to distortions, cracks, or even gaps. These issues can obstruct your view of the road, increasing the risk of accidents. To ensure your windshield doesn't compromise your safety, it's imperative to have it installed correctly by professionals.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to install a windshield?

The time required for windshield installation varies, but it typically takes about one to two hours. Factors like the type of vehicle and the complexity of the installation can affect the duration.

Can I drive immediately after a windshield is installed?

In most cases, it's recommended to wait at least one hour after installation before driving. This allows the adhesive to cure properly, ensuring the windshield's stability.

What should I look for in a professional windshield installer?

When choosing a professional, make sure they are certified and experienced in windshield installation. Additionally, inquire about the type of adhesive they use, as high-quality adhesive is crucial for a secure installation.

Is windshield installation covered by insurance?

Many insurance policies cover windshield replacement or repair. However, coverage can vary, so it's essential to check with your insurance provider to understand your specific policy.

Can a small crack in the windshield be repaired instead of replaced?

In some cases, small chips or cracks can be repaired rather than replaced. However, the decision depends on the size, location, and severity of the damage. A professional can assess whether repair is possible.

How can I maintain my windshield after installation?

To extend the lifespan of your windshield, avoid using abrasive cleaners, park in the shade when possible to reduce exposure to UV rays, and address small cracks or chips promptly to prevent them from spreading.
